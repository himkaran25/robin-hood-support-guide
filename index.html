Quick Steps to Reach Support

1
Try the in-app help first
Open Robinhood app → Account (person icon) → Help. Many common issues can be resolved here quickly.
2
Call the support line
If you need to speak with someone, use the phone number below.
3
Prepare account details
Have your account email, full name, and last 4 digits of SSN ready to speed verification.
4
Escalation & follow-up
If the first agent can't resolve it, politely request an escalation or a case number so you can follow up.

Frequently asked

Q: Is phone the fastest option?
A: For account verification and urgent issues, phone is typically the fastest way to get a human. For non-urgent items, in-app message may be fine.
Q: What if I can't access my account?
A: Use the "Forgot password" flow in the app or mention the account lock when calling +1 (866) 654-0069 so the agent can prioritize verification.

Contact & Safety

Other tips:

  • Never share full passwords in messages.
  • Robinhood will never ask for your full SSN over chat.
  • Document any case number or agent name for follow-up.
